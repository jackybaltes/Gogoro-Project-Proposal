Scientific Goals

  • Balancing on two wheels
  • Perception for autonomous driving on the test course: lines, railway crossing, turns
  • Motion planning for control of existing scooter
  • Path planning for Dubin's car in complex environments

Milestones

1 Modify Thormang hardware and improve shoulder joints and grippers for safe control of scooter

2 Start and stop on two wheels. Balancing on two wheels at various speeds

3 Path planning and control for varied paths

4 Combine skills to pass scooter licence test
